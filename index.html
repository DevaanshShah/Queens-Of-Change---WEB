Mission

Half the world bleeds in silence. We refuse to. Queens of Change exists to break the stigma around menstruation and stand beside every girl, every woman, every quiet act of courage — with pads, with knowledge, with the unshakable belief that dignity is not a luxury.

Our Approach

Quiet work. Loud impact.

  1. 01

    We meet women where they are

    In villages, schools, shelters, and slums — we don't wait for the world to come to us. We walk in, sit down, and listen.

  2. 02

    We replace shame with knowledge

    Every conversation, every workshop turns whispered fears into shared facts. Menstruation is biology, not embarrassment.

  3. 03

    We deliver dignity, monthly

    Over 2,00,000 sanitary pads handed directly into the hands that need them — no middlemen, no judgement, no cost.

  4. 04

    We build leaders, not dependents

    Skills training, mentorship, and leadership labs that turn recipients into ambassadors. Today's volunteer is tomorrow's chapter head.

Programs

Four pillars. One mission.

/ 01

Sanitary Pad Distribution

Complimentary pads delivered directly to marginalized girls and women. Over 2,00,000 distributed and counting.

/ 02

Health Education

Workshops that demystify menstruation, reproductive health, and consent — taught with warmth, never with whispers.

/ 03

Women Empowerment

Skills training, mentorship, and leadership labs that turn quiet strength into loud, lasting self-reliance.

/ 04

Community Outreach

Listening tours in schools, villages, and shelters — because real change starts with real conversations.
